Why Choose US

Because we enable direct pet adoption, from one good home to another.

Kind To Everyone


We belive that every pet deserves to be safe, loved and respected

people who are great candidates for adoption shouldn't be put off by complicated processes or one-size-fits-all rules

people who need to rehome their pets should be empowered to do so without being judged

Responsible Rehoming


We’re champions of rehoming. But not at any cost.

We believe in finding the right match between adopters and pets, not taking risks or rushing.

We always prioritise pet welfare. And we offer a safer, more ethical and professional alternative to online marketplaces like Preloved, Pets4Homes, Facebook and Gumtree.

Advocate Adoption


This value sits at the heart of everything we do.

Adoption reduces the demand for puppy farming, industrial-scale breeding, illegal pet imports and other forms of exploitation and abuse.

We’re proud supporters of #AdoptDontShop.

Who We Are

Our mission is to find forever loving families and homes for animals in need.We home/rehome orphaned, abandoned, homeless cats & dogs from the streets after providing them with state-of-the-art private medical care to ensure a hassle-free experience and life for the adopters as well as the pet.

IN Addition

We get community street dogs sterilized to keep the population in check.

Educate adopter families on the importance of sterilization.

Feed stray dogs in the neighbouring areas.

Regularly follow-up with adopters to ensure the well-being of the pet adopted from us.

reat injured animals and release them back into their territories. If we they cannot survive on their own, we get them adopted.

Facilitate rehoming and adoption of animals in areas of low adoption rates/chances.

Our team has independently homed 700+ Pups, Kittens and Dogs in the last 4 years, including several blind/deaf kittens and handicapped dogs. We also closely work with other pet adoption groups to help them with their adoption activities.

OUR MISSION

To inform and educate the community while saving the animals in need: one hand, one paw, one voice.

What Do We Get Out of This?

A heart filled with content for being born as a “Human Being”! Pure joy and happiness! No words can express the satisfaction and contentment we get when a pet gets adopted and grows with the family with love and care!

We love animals and will work tirelessly (and intelligently) to reduce their suffering and agony. While we shelter animals, feed them and care for their injuries, our primary goal is to work towards minimizing animals being born unwanted and living miserably.
