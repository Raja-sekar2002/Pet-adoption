What We Do

SHELTER

Our animal shelter at Kovalam is a very spacious, airy, and healthy environment on the beach front that houses 190+ animals.These dogs and cats are too old, or weak, or have a permanent injury like paralysis. Our shelters are safe havens where they can live out the rest of their days in peace. We try to place as many animals as possible into responsible and loving homes

FEEDING

All scavenging strays are undernourished and suffer from ill-health. Most of them have skin diseases. During the initial lockdown of March - April 2020, the street animals were cut off from their main sources of food, as eateries, restaurants, hotels, and butcher shops were closed. We noticed their pathetic condition and started feeding starving strays in the areas of – Muthukaadu and Kovalam

TREATMENT

We treat conditions such as mange, wounds, injuries, and other diseases found in the stray animals. We take the animals to the best veterinary doctors in the city for advanced surgeries

ANIMAL BIRTH CONTROL CENTER

We have converted an old shipping container into a hospital that can perform birth control surgeries for street dogs in the Greater Chennai area. We aim to establish this facility that can sterilize 1000 animals every month. The hospital and kennel infrastructure can be shifted to new locations
